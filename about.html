About

I am a software and aerospace engineer who enjoys working at the boundary between software, hardware, controls, data, and physical system behavior. My background is not limited to one narrow layer of a product; I like understanding how the full system works and how each engineering decision affects the final result.

Professionally, I have worked on C++ and Python software for hardware-connected systems, including low-latency algorithms, modular architecture, automation, test infrastructure, SQL-backed data workflows, CI/CD pipelines, and integration work. I also have hands-on experience building backend and cloud-connected software, including scheduled automation, API-based data generation, user-data handling, and cloud-hosted workflows.

I also care about technical leadership. I have supported software engineers as a product owner and technical lead, and previously helped lead a large aerospace engineering org. My goal is to keep building reliable systems while staying flexible enough to learn the surrounding disciplines needed to solve the actual problem.
